Why Cata

Social media for the life you're actually building.

i.
✦

Set your catas

Define any goal in any domain — fitness, reading, a creative habit, a skill. Add your "why" and the app turns it into a daily practice with a target to work toward.

ii.
◎

Watch momentum build

One tap to check in. Each week becomes a circle, each day a dot — filled, missed, or ahead. You can see your progress at a glance, and so can your circle.

iii.
◉

Your circle, passively

Friends' momentum appears quietly in your feed. No likes, no follower counts. Just the people you actually know, showing up alongside you every day.

Momentum grid

Every day is a dot. Every week is a circle.

Most apps show you a number. Cata shows you the shape of your effort — weeks stacking up into something you can see and feel.

A full circle means a perfect week. A partial circle means you showed up. Both matter. The grid doesn't lie — and it doesn't judge. It just shows you where you've been and how far you're going.

How it works

Two loops. One life.

01
Daily habit loop

Set a cata

Pick any goal, add your "why", and set a target — 21 days, 28 days, 30. The app turns it into a daily practice with a momentum grid waiting to be filled.

02
Daily habit loop

Check in daily

One tap. Your dot fills in. Your week circle grows. A notification reminds you by evening if you haven't checked in — momentum is yours to protect.

03
Social loop

Your circle sees it

Friends passively see your momentum — no posting required. Accountability happens without anyone having to ask. You can send a nudge when a friend goes quiet.

04
Social loop

Read the brief

Each day, a short excerpt from a journal, speech, or memoir — Marcus Aurelius, Maya Angelou, Viktor Frankl — with a reflection prompt tied to your goals.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2025

Cata ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile information: display name and optional profile photo
  • Goal and activity data: the catas you set, your daily check-in history, and momentum data
  • Contact information: if you choose to connect with friends, we access your contacts only with your explicit permission
  • Communications: messages or feedback you send to us

We also collect certain information automatically when you use the Service, including device type, operating system version, app version, and usage analytics.

How we use your information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Send you check-in reminders and push notifications (which you can disable at any time)
  • Enable the social features of the app, such as showing your friends' momentum in their feed
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve the user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: for example, when you connect with friends on the platform
  • Service providers: we use third-party vendors who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

Your goal data and momentum history are visible only to you and the friends you explicitly add. We never share your personal goals publicly.

Data retention

We retain your account information and activity data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app's settings, which will permanently delete all associated data within 30 days.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Children's privacy

Cata is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

Frequently asked questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data within 30 days.

Can I recover a missed day?

Yes — Cata includes rest days and a grace period mechanic so one missed day doesn't erase your momentum. Check the settings in your cata to configure rest day preferences.

Who can see my catas?

Only friends you explicitly add can see your momentum. Your goals are never visible publicly, and you can remove a friend at any time.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications to customize or disable check-in reminders. You can also manage permissions through your iPhone's Settings app.

Is Cata free?

Cata is free to download and use. Core functionality will always remain free.
